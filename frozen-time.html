Meaning of the Work

"FROZEN TIME" is a cinematic work by Dominik Šuškevič, created for the "Ąžuolynas" International Film Festival. Although the initial creative phase was intuitive, later, as the work took its final shape, the author personally realized the deep meaning of this film. The piece became not only a visual project but also a personal discovery of what frozen time and its emotional value truly mean.

Filmed in the surroundings of Cirkliškis Manor, the film explores the relationship between the frozen winter and enduring memories. It is a story about seeking warmth in the coldest moments – through connection with family, loved ones, and the perfection of nature. The symbolic tree at the end of the video marks an emotional rebirth and the blooming of hope within a person.

"The most important thing is not what you see, but what you feel when time stands still."
