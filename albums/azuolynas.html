Street | Ąžuolynas

Kaunas Ąžuolynas is the largest urban park of old oaks in Europe, covering an area of over 80 hectares in the Žaliakalnis district. This series captures unique shots where the century-old peace of nature and the long-lasting aura of the trees intertwine with the daily dynamics of the city. It is a space where the rhythm of the street meets the greatness of nature.
